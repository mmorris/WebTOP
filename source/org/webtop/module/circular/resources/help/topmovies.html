Fresnel diffraction from a circular aperture: change of the diameter of the aperture. The wavelength and position of the observation screen are held constant and the diameter of the aperture is varied. The initial and final Fresnel numbers are 1.00 and 5.00, respectively.

Fresnel diffraction from a circular aperture: change of the wavelength. The slit width and position of the observation screen are held constant and the wavelength is varied. The initial and final Fresnel numbers are 4.00 and 2.29, respectively.
