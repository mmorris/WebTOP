The images below depict the irradiance patterns produced when a monochromatic plane wave of wave length '' is normally incident upon a circular aperture of diameter 'D'. 'z' is the distance from the aperture point to the observation point. The Fresnel number, NF , is defined as

NF = D2 /4z
