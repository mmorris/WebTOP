 1. Description

Consider a unit amplitude, monochromatic plane wave of wavelength traveling in the positive z-direction. Let us suppose that this wave is normally incident upon an opaque screen that occupies the plane z = 0 and has a circular aperture of radius a which is centered about the origin.
