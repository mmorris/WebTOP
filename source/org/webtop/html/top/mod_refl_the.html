Theory of Reflection and Refraction

 1. Description

Consider two homogeneous nonmagnetic media, which have a planar interface. The "incident" medium occupies the half-space y > 0 and has index of refraction . The "transmission" medium occupies the half-space y < 0 and has index of refraction . In this module the reflection and refraction of monochromatic electromagnetic plane waves at this interface is investigated. For simplicity only the electric field (and not the magnetic field) is considered.
