Diffraction - Fraunhofer N-Slit: Theory

Introduction

In this module monochromatic light of wavelength l is normally incident upon a plane that contains N slits of width w and center-to-center separation d. The resulting intensity pattern is viewed on an observation screen that is a distance z from the aperture plane.  Under normal operation, the WebTOP window should look like Fig. 1 below.
