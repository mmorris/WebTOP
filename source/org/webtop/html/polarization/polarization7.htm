Relevant Outside Links

When considering polarizers, one might ask how does it actually work. Developers of the Physics 2000 project at the University of Colorado at Boulder have developed an applet that simulates what a hydrocarbon molecule does as it absorbs light. You may view the applet by clicking: Hydrocarbon Simulation

Another interesting applet by the same group illustrates the use of a common day polarizer. You may reproduce the effects illustrated in the applet by using lenses from regular polarized sun glasses. Here is the applet: Polarizers

You are encouraged to check the whole Polarization module at Physics 2000. The URL is: http://www.colorado.edu/physics/2000/polarization/index.html
