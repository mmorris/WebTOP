Reflection and Refraction - Waves Two Media: Theory

Introduction

This module simulates the reflection and refraction of an s-polarized, monochromatic plane wave of light incident upon a planar interface between two nonmagnetic, dielectric media. The media occupy the half spaces x < 0 and x > 0, and the plane of incidence is the x-y plane. The module depicts, as the height in a “wave pool,” the total electric field on each side of the interface, for observation points in the plane of incidence (see Fig. 1 below). The module also has options that allow only incident wave or only the reflected wave in the region x < 0 to be displayed.
Figure 1 The Reflection and Refraction/Waves Two Media module. The white vectors are the incident, reflected, and transmitted wave vectors. The widget on the left controls the parameters of the incident electric field.
