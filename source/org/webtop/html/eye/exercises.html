Exercise 1:  Corrective Lens for a Myopic Eye

On the Setup panel on the Set vision line, click on the Near-Sighted (Myopic) button. This makes the eye myopic, and its far point is 60.00 cm from the eye.
(a) If lens is to be placed 1.3 cm from the eye, calculate what the power of the lens (in diopters) must be in order for this eye to see far away objects clearly.
(b) Click on the Corrective Optics tab. Click on the Add button, then click on the Set button. The required lens power is displayed in the Power keyboard entry box. Does your answer agree with this value?

Exercise 2: Contact Lens for a Myopic Eye

On the Setup panel on the Set vision line, click on the Near-Sighted (Myopic) button. This makes the eye myopic, and its far point is 60.00 cm from the eye.
(a) If a contact lens is placed on the eye, calculate what the power of the lens (in diopters) must be in order for this eye to see far away objects clearly.
(b) Click on the Corrective Optics tab. Click on the Add button, then click on the Contacts button, then click on the Set button. The required lens power is displayed in the Power keyboard entry box. Does your answer agree with this value?
